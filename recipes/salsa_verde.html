Tomatillo Salsa Verde

How to make Tomatillo salsa

Making tomatillo salsa is incredibly easy: Bring all the ingredients to a boil, simmer until the tomatillos are soft, and puree the mixture until smooth

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 serrano chile pepper, minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 cup water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place tomatillos, onion, chile pepper, and garlic into a large saucepan; season with cilantro, oregano, salt, and cumin. Pour in water and bring to a boil over high heat; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until the tomatillos are soft, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Transfer tomatillo mixture into a blender in batches; puree until smooth.
  4. serve with chips
